🛡️ AI Policy Framework

✅ Encouraged Uses

  • • Lesson planning and activity brainstorming
  • • Creating rubrics and assessments
  • • Generating feedback templates
  • • Professional development and reflection
  • • Parent communication drafting
  • • Classroom management strategies

❌ Prohibited Uses

  • • Inputting student identifying information
  • • Sharing confidential school/family data
  • • Creating final grades without human review
  • • Replacing genuine teacher-student interactions

⚠️ Use with Caution

  • • Always review AI suggestions before implementing
  • • Adapt content for your specific students and context
  • • Verify factual information, especially in science/social studies
  • • Never replace your professional judgment with AI recommendations

🔒 Privacy Requirements

  • • Use generic examples: "A student in my class..." not names
  • • No photos with student faces visible
  • • Log out of accounts on shared devices
  • • Never save student-specific prompts
